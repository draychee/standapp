An App to help guide Scrums for personal use



  • Build your own Team

    Create as many teams as you would like, adding members with shared details and Progresses


  • Keep track of your Progress

    Keep track of your Accomplishments, current tasks and potential blockers so that you know how to prioritize your next steps.


  • Manage your Team, Standups and Progresses

    Members of each team will be able to add, edit and delete either Teams, Standups or Progressses.
